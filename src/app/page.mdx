## Objectives

- Master the core components of web development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
- Explore cutting-edge frameworks and technologies: Node.js, React.js.
- Gain practical experience in OpenAI and AI integration.
- Develop a personalized AI-powered website as your capstone project.

Your journey will culminate in a final project: creating an AI website that showcases your creativity and the technical skills you've acquired. This project is your opportunity to bring your vision to life, demonstrating your proficiency in web development and AI integration.
